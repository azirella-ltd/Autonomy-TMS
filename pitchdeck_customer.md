## Slide 7: Four Pillars — Why Autonomy Works

### Each pillar reinforces the others — a self-reinforcing advantage that gets stronger with every decision.

### 1. Autonomous AI Agents
Eleven specialized agents operate as a coordinated team at each site. Each agent makes narrow, focused decisions — ATP allocation, purchase orders, inventory rebalancing, quality management — at machine speed, 24/7. Every decision is explainable and overrideable.

### 2. Conformal Prediction
Every agent decision carries a **mathematically calibrated confidence score** — not a guess, not a heuristic. Coverage guarantees hold regardless of your data distribution. When confidence drops, decisions automatically escalate for human review. This is what makes autonomous operation trustworthy.

### 3. Causal AI
The only rigorous way to know if a decision *worked*. Counterfactual reasoning compares what happened to what *would* have happened — separating good decisions from lucky outcomes. Your system learns from skill, not coincidence.

### 4. ERP-Specific Digital Twin
A complete simulation of your supply chain, calibrated from your actual ERP data. Generates thousands of scenarios for agent training and confidence calibration. Your agents learn on a digital copy of *your* business before they act on the real thing.
