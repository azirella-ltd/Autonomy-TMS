# AWS ISV Partner Strategy — Autonomy Platform

**Date**: 2026-03-18 | **Status**: Research / Pre-Enrollment

---

## Why AWS Partnership

Autonomy implements **100% of the AWS Supply Chain Data Model** (35/35 entities). AWS Supply Chain (the product) provides visibility and insights but is NOT a planning/execution platform. Autonomy fills that gap — making it a natural complement, not a competitor.

**Strategic alignment:**
- AWS pushing **Agentic AI** competencies (3 new categories 2025) — Autonomy's 11 TRM agents + GNN + Claude Skills fit perfectly
- Mid-market manufacturers (Autonomy's target) are already AWS customers frustrated with Kinaxis/SAP costs
- Autonomy runs natively on AWS infrastructure (Docker/PostgreSQL on EC2, RDS, or ECS)
- AWS sellers get **quota retirement credit** for co-selling ISV Accelerate solutions — financial incentive to sell Autonomy

## Enrollment Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

**Step 1: Register with AWS Partner Network (APN)**
- URL: https://partnercentral.awspartner.com/APNSelfRegister
- Enroll in the **Software Path**
- **Cost**: $2,500/year APN fee
- Creates your AWS Partner Central account

**Step 2: Complete Foundational Technical Review (FTR)**
- AWS reviews your architecture against Well-Architected Framework
- Autonomy's strengths: multi-tenant isolation, PostgreSQL on RDS, Docker/ECS compatibility
- Addressing 25%+ of high-risk findings unlocks Well-Architected ISV Funding credits
- This is the critical gate — everything downstream requires FTR completion

**Step 3: List on AWS Marketplace**
- Package Autonomy as **SaaS** (preferred) or **AMI**
- SaaS: customers subscribe via Marketplace, Autonomy hosts on AWS
- AMI: customer deploys in their own VPC (Docker Compose → AMI conversion)
- Requires: Marketplace Seller account + Amazon Payee Central (for payouts)
- Listing must be **General Availability** (not beta/preview)

### Phase 2: Build Pipeline (Months 2-6)

**Step 4: Achieve ACE Program Eligibility**
- ACE = APN Customer Engagements — AWS's co-sell pipeline tool
- Register opportunities (prospects, demos, POCs) in ACE
- **Target**: 15 qualified opportunities in 12 months (ISV Accelerate requirement)

**Step 5: Generate Launched Opportunities**
- Convert ACE opportunities to launched deals (closed-won or active POC)
- **Target**: 5 launched opportunities in 12 months
- These can be AWS Marketplace Private Offers or direct sales with AWS co-sell

**Step 6: Complete Co-Selling Training**
- "Co-Selling with AWS" learning module (online, self-paced)
- At least 1 team member must complete

### Phase 3: ISV Accelerate Enrollment (Month 6+)

**Step 7: Meet All 8 Requirements**

| # | Requirement | Status |
|---|------------|--------|
| 1 | Validated/Differentiated Software Path | Phase 1 |
| 2 | Active Marketplace listing (GA) | Phase 1 |
| 3 | ACE Program eligibility | Phase 2 |
| 4 | Linked Partner Central ↔ Marketplace | Phase 1 |
| 5 | 5 launched opportunities (12 months) | Phase 2 |
| 6 | 15 qualified opportunities in ACE (12 months) | Phase 2 |
| 7 | Co-selling training complete | Phase 2 |
| 8 | ≥$2,000 recognized AWS Account revenue | Phase 2 |

**Step 8: Receive ISVA Invitation**
- Automatic when requirements are met — ISVA team sends enrollment task
- Assign AWS Partner Development resource for co-sell strategy

### Phase 4: Specialization (Month 9+)

**Step 9: Pursue AWS AI Competency**
- 3 new Agentic AI categories (2025): Applications, Tools, Consulting
- Autonomy fits **Applications** (agentic SC planning)
- Partners with specialization badges get:
  - 50% more Marketing Development Funds
  - Dedicated badge visible in Marketplace search results
  - Higher ranking in customer search

**Step 10: Pursue Supply Chain Specialization (if available)**
- No specific SC competency exists yet — opportunity to be first
- AWS may create one as SC AI grows — early mover advantage
